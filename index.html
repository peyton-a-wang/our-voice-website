TEAM

Emily Pattison

CEO

Emily is a senior at Wellesley College double-majoring in Media Sciences and Economics. With a background in UX design, media production, and coding, Emily is able to apply her studies to Our Voice.

Having studied Economics abroad at Oxford University and interning at Cartesian, doing data and strategy consulting work for AT&T, Emily also brings strong quantitative analysis to the team.

Past internships at the ACLU of New Hampshire, Family Friendly Economy, and NH Together have provided her experience in grassroots organizing and activism.

As a video producer and director for The Mars Generation, Emily has also produced video and social media content for clients like The LEGO Group, Nissan USA, Disney, Eggo, and more.

As a scholar with Kode with Klossy, Ana learned web development with CSS, HTML, and Javascript, giving her a technical understanding to apply to Our Voice.
